AKAN NAMING SYSTEM

The system allows the user to feed their birthday and it calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.



Origin - Ghana, West Africa

The naming system among the traditional Ghanaian culture revolved around the day of the week when a child was born and the gender of the child. Anyone around the world can use this system to check their day of the week when they were born, and if they were Ghanaian, what their possible name would be.
